What is LionList?

LionList is a like a mix between Craigslist, CULPA, and the Columbia University Facebook group. Come here to connect with other CU students who are selling the things you want, and shopping for the things you're selling.

End Facebook Spam

Imagine a world where your news feed isn't mobbed by people trying to buy and sell stuff at the beginning and end of every semester. With LionList, shop when you want to, and advertise the things you are selling to people who actually want to buy them.

Organize. Win.

Never sift through Facebook posts looking for "that one guy selling that one book" again. Posts on LionList are organized by category, and all book posts are organized by department and course, so you can see exactly what books are being bought and sold by other students for any class at any time.

Discover

Books aren't the only thing students want! Buy and sell appliances, dorm furniture, concert tickets, tutoring services or language classes, and more from your fellow Columbians over LionList as well.
